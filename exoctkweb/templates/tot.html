Transit Observation Tools

The TOT is a transiting exoplanet noise simulatior. The current implementation scales the measured flux, variance, and exposure time values from previously-observed systems, computes the expected rms per spectrophotometric channel, and estimates the transit/eclipse depth error based on the anticiapted number of valid in-and-out-of-transit data points. The uncertainty estimates depend on the orbital properites of the system, instrument configuration, and observation duration.
